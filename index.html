Fitness Assesment

Fitness assessments consist of different types of tests and exercises used to determine your overall health and physical fitness level. These tests typically assess your strength, endurance, and flexibility.

The basic types of fitness assessments include body composition, muscular strength, muscular endurance, cardiorespiratory fitness, and flexibility.

Group Training

The term group fitness emcompasses any and all form of fitness that's done in a group setting,lead by a personal trainer or group instructor.This category includes "Zumba","Step Aerobics" and "BodyPump".

Sport-Specific Training

Sport specific training is simply fitness and performance training designed specifically for athletic perfoemance enhancement.This training includes sprinting,box jumps and squat jumps.
